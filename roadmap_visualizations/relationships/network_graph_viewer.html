Legend

  • Programs: Space missions and projects
  • Products: Components and systems being developed
  • Material Systems: Materials used in products
  • Printing Suppliers: Suppliers providing 3D printing services
  • Post-Processing Suppliers: Suppliers providing post-processing services
  • Funding Opportunities: Sources of funding for tasks

Relationship Types

  • Solid lines: Direct relationships (e.g., a product is used in a program)
  • Dashed lines: Post-processing relationships
  • Dotted lines: Funding relationships

Use the interactive controls to explore the network:

  • Pan: Click and drag to move around
  • Zoom: Use mouse wheel or zoom tools
  • Hover: Hover over nodes to see details
  • Reset: Reset the view to default
